MAINTENANCE

Sneaker Care Tips

Keep your kicks fresh with these essential care techniques

  • Use a soft brush to remove dirt and debris
  • Clean white soles with a mix of baking soda and water
  • Stuff shoes with paper to maintain shape when not in use
  • Apply a water and stain repellent spray for protection
